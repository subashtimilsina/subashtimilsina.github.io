Project · ABU Robocon 2018, Vietnam

Autonomous Mecanum Drive Robot with Multi-sensor Navigation

Robotics Sensor Fusion ABU Robocon

Task

Built for the ABU Robocon 2018 competition held in Vietnam. The contest required teams to design autonomous and manual robots that could complete a sequence of throwing and tossing tasks under tight time limits.

My Role

I worked primarily on the navigation and mechanisms of the autonomous robot — designing the mecanum-drive base for omnidirectional motion, integrating multiple sensors (encoders, IMU, ultrasonic) for robust closed-loop motion, and tuning the control loops for accurate positioning on the field.

  • Mecanum-wheel kinematics and inverse kinematics for omnidirectional drive.
  • Sensor fusion of encoders, IMU, and field markers for pose estimation.
  • State-machine controller for sequencing the autonomous task pipeline.
